What We Do

Our focus is on being up-to-date and in contact with professors and experts in our research fields.
In each seminar series, speakers present their research and scientific activities and articles written by themselves.
The participants, whether in person or online, can participate in the meeting and have access to the published files.
During the seminar and at the end, there will be questions and answers, and at the end there is a possibility to communicate with the speaker and have a conversation with them.
